Mohammed Abdel-Raouf Arafat al-Qudwa al-Husseini is popularly known as
Yasser Arafat.  He received the Nobel Peace Prize in 1994.

Mikhail Gorbachev was the last leader of the Soviet Union.  He received the
Nobel Peace Price in 1990.  Boris Yeltsin was the first president of the
Russian Federation.  

Jimmy Carter was the 39th President of the United States.  Ronald Reagan
was the 40th President of the United States.

Margaret Thatcher is the only woman to have held the office of Prime Minister
of the United Kingdom.

Bob Hawke was the 23rd Prime Minister of Australia.  Paul Keating was the
24th.

Mother Teresa, an Albanian Roman Catholic nun, won the Nobel Peace Prize in
1979.
